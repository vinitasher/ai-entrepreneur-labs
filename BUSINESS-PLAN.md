## Revenue Model

- **Primary:** Digital product sales (one-time purchases)
- **Secondary:** Email list → product launches, affiliate commissions
- **Tertiary:** Consulting/implementation services

## Cost Structure

- **Gumroad fees:** 10% per sale
- **Domain hosting:** ~$10/year (GitHub Pages or Cloudflare)
- **AI API costs:** ~$5-20/month (for content creation)
- **Total monthly overhead:** ~$20-50

**Break-even:** ~3-5 sales

## Compounding Factors

1. **SEO compounds** — blog posts drive traffic for years
2. **Email list compounds** — each new subscriber is a future customer
3. **Product line compounds** — cross-selling between products
4. **Social proof compounds** — reviews and testimonials build trust
5. **Content compounds** — free guide → paid products → repeat customers

## Marketing Strategy

1. **SEO content** — long-tail keywords for AI automation guides
2. **Free guide** — email capture → nurture sequence → paid products
3. **Social media** — daily tips and automation examples
4. **Community** — Reddit, Hacker News, Product Hunt launches
5. **Affiliates** — share revenue with promoters

## Competitive Advantage

- Practical > theoretical (most competitors teach concepts, we provide tools)
- Tested in real-world use before publishing
- Affordable pricing with free tier
- Compounding product ecosystem
